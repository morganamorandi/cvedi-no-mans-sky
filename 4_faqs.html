Services and assistance

Once your journey has been completed, you may claim compensation independently within 1 year of the date on which the delay occurred or the expiration of the season ticket (pass or other cards) used, in the following ways:

  • at the travel agency that issued the ticket;
  • at any ticket office;
  • via the Call Center, only for tickets purchased at Nexus.

The assistance service is provided by our crossroad and is addressed to:

  • individuals in a wheelchair because of illness or disability;
  • individuals with limb problems or with difficulty in walking;
  • elderly people;
  • pregnant women;
  • blind or visually impaired people;
  • deaf or hearing impaired people;
  • people with mental handicap.

If you lost one of your items you can go to Lost&Found Office. You can find the directions for the Lost&Found Office in the Map & Directions section.
When a lost item is found, it is handed over to the airport's Lost&Found Office and you'll receive an e-mail asking you to confirm that the item found is yours and start the process of shipping it by courier or picking it up at the airport. It may take 2 days to 1 week between the time the item is found and the time you receive the notification.
The collection can be done by making an appointment through the Call Center or sending a e-mail as instructed in the discovery confirmation.
In the event of collection by a proxy, the following documents must be presented/send:

  • a proxy for collection stating the name of the delegated person;
  • a photocopy of your ID;
  • a photocopy of the identification document of the person delegated to collect the item.

If you wish to receive your item through shipping you'll have to fill in the shipment form. The item will be sent by the chosen courier to the postal address provided and the shipping cost will be calculated based on the destination.

If you need to contact our personnel you can find click on the "call for assistance" button at the bottom of this page or:

  • Contact our call center, operative 7 days a week from 8am to 10pm NST at 0948 473858;
  • Contact us writing to our e-mail address nexus.assistance@hub.nexus.
