Services and assistance

  • When and how to claim compensation for a ride delay?

    Once your journey has been completed, you may claim compensation independently within 1 year of the date on which the delay occurred or the expiration of the season ticket (pass or other cards) used, in the following ways

    • here, only for tickets that have been purchased online, using the "Request Compensation" feature, which will be available after you have accessed your journey detail here, or from your Customer Area;
    • at the travel agency that issued the ticket;
    • at any ticket office;
    • via the Call Center, only for tickets purchased at Nexus.

  • Assistance to person with disabilities or reduced mobility

    The assistance service is provided by our crossroad and is addressed to:

    • individuals in a wheelchair because of illness or disability;
    • individuals with limb problems or with difficulty in walking;
    • elderly people;
    • pregnant women;
    • blind or visually impaired people;
    • deaf or hearing impaired people;
    • people with mental handicap.
